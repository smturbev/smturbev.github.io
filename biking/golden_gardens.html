Golden Gardens

Notes:

  • Starting Point: UW Rainier Vista
  • Relatively flat and easy out and back
  • Highly trafficked, don't go during rush hour
  • Portion on the road in Ballard because they still haven't connected the Burke through Ballard >:(
  • Distance: ~15 mi roundtrip
  • Estimated Time: ~1.5 hrs

    • You'll definitely want to budget in 30 min or so to hang out at the beach at Golden Gardens because it is gorgeous! This is a great ride for getting started. Low effort, high reward.
