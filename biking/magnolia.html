Magnolia Loop (and bonus add-on)

Magnolia Loop

Notes:

  • Starting Point: Fremont bridge
  • Big up hill right before the amazing view in Discovery
  • Small portion on the road with light car-traffic
  • Distance: ~18 mi roundtrip
  • Estimated Time: ~1.5 hours

    • Beautiful vista through Discovery Park and along the waterfront. Definitely need to build up your hill skills before attempting this route.
