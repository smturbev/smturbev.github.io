Bellevue Loop (520 to I-90)

Notes:

  • Starting Point: UW Rainier Vista
  • Some hills
  • Large portion on neighborhood greenways, light car traffic
  • Distance: ~23 mi roundtrip
  • Estimated Time: ~2 hours

    • The I-90 bridge is a bit nerve-wracking as a person who worries about things falling through the crack between the concrete and the railing. It is also very noisy from the cars speeding by on the freeway. I also always get slightly lost on Mercer Island because the path there is fairly winding with lots of street crossings. Other than that, there are gorgeous view from Bellevue and it was a pleasant ride.
